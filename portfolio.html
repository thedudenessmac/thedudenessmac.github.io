INT. ABOUT ME — MORNING

Hello and welcome! My name is Marko. I'm a musician. A writer. A cook. An educator. A photographer. A plain-old human being.

And I am also a SCRIPT SUPERVISOR.

My job, in the simplest sense, is to protect the integrity of the artistic vision that sustains every narrative project, be it a short film, feature, or series.

Or, put another way: I am a cost-containment measure for the production at large. I solve problems BEFORE they become EXPENSIVE problems days, weeks, or months down the line.

Truth be told, these are really two sides of the same coin.
