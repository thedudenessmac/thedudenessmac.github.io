About

Hello and welcome. My name is Marko. I'm a musician, a writer, a cook, an educator, a photographer, a tech geek, and a plain old human being.

I am also a Script Supervisor.

My job is to protect the integrity of the artistic vision that sustains every narrative project, be it a short film, feature, or series.

Or, put more dispassionately, I am a cost-containment measure for the production at large. I solve problems BEFORE they become EXPENSIVE problems days, weeks, or months down the road.
