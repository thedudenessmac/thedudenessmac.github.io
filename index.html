About

Hello and welcome. My name is Marko. I'm a musician, a writer, a cook, an educator, a photographer, a tech geek, and a plain-old human being.

And I am also a Script Supervisor.

My job, most simply put, is to protect the integrity of the artistic vision that sustains every narrative project — be it a short film, feature, or series.

Or, put another way: I am a cost-containment measure for the production at large. I solve problems before they become expensive problems days, weeks, or months down the road.

Truth be told, these are really two sides of the same coin.
